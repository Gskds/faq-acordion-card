FAQ

How many team members can I invite?
You can invite up to 5 team members on the free plan. Premium plans allow up to 20 team members.
What is the maximum file upload size?
No more than 2GB. All files in your account must fit your allotted storage space.
How do I reset my password?
Go to your account settings, click on "Security", and then "Reset Password". You'll receive an email with instructions.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your account will remain active until the end of your billing cycle.
Do you provide additional support?
Yes, we offer 24/7 customer support via chat and email. Premium users also have access to phone support.
